About Luca’s Loaves

We currently only have one local store, where classic breads are combined with a selection of local ingredients. I would like to share with you my passion for bread, pastries, sweet pastries and some salty pastries...... We currently have five stores in France and nearly 60 stores in Asia, the Americas, Saudi Arabia and Australia. In our shop, classic French breads are combined with a selection of local ingredients. I would like to share with you my passion for bread, pastries, sweet pastries and some salty pastries......

Every loaf, pastry, sweet bread in the store, whether inland or on the other side of the globe, tells the story of my journey: a meeting, an exchange, a sharing or a daring attempt......
